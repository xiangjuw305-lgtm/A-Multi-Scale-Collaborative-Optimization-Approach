Precision (P):
The proportion of predicted positive detections that are correctly matched with ground-truth objects.

Recall (R):
The proportion of ground-truth objects that are correctly detected by the model.

mAP@0.5:
Mean Average Precision calculated at an IoU threshold of 0.5.

mAP@0.5:0.95:
Mean Average Precision averaged over multiple IoU thresholds from 0.5 to 0.95.

Parameters:
The number of model parameters, reported in millions.

GFLOPs:
Giga Floating-Point Operations under the same input resolution.
